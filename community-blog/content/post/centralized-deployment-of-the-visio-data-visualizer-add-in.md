To provide a quick recap, the Visio Data Visualizer add-in for Excel
enables you to create high quality Visio diagrams---including basic
flowcharts, cross-functional flowcharts (or swim lane diagrams), and
organizational charts---from data directly in Excel.

 

The add-in was released for [public
preview](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/visio-blog/data-visualizer-add-in-for-excel-now-available-in-public-preview/ba-p/973962)
to all Office 365 subscribers in November 2019 and is available through
the [Office
Store](https://appsource.microsoft.com/product/office/WA104381973). No
Visio subscription is required to use the add-in.

 

To address one of the most popular questions from users since the public
preview announcement, we're providing you with an overview of how to
deploy the add-in using the admin center and Centralized
Deployment---the recommended way for admins to deploy add-ins to users
via a group or to everyone in the tenant.

 

Note: If, as an end user, you do not have access to the [Office
Store](https://appsource.microsoft.com/marketplace/apps), reach out to
your tenant admin. Access to the Office Store and its add-ins can be
turned on or off in the admin center by selecting **User owned apps and
services** under the **Settings** \> **Services & add-ins** page.

 

[This
document](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/admin/manage/manage-deployment-of-add-ins?view=o365-worldwide#deploy-an-office-add-in-using-the-admin-center)
provides more details about how to deploy an Office add-in using the
admin center and how to turn on add-in acquisition for everyone in a
tenant. Please see below for a quick look at the Centralized Deployment
feature.
