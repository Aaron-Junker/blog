[[![](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/44139iF581C5AE6450A9D1/image-size/large?v=v2&px=999){.lia-media-image

Sometimes when you reposition those shapes, you may end up with an
undesirable jagged connector like this:

[[![](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/44140i8150DF0B26224CE2/image-size/large?v=v2&px=999){.lia-media-image

connection points on the shapes. To prevent this from happening, Visio
has a feature called [dynamic
glue](http://blogs.msdn.com/b/visio/archive/2006/09/15/better-layout-using-dynamic-glue.aspx)
that lets connectors automatically reroute to the nearest pair of
