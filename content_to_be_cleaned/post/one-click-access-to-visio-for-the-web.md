The Visio for the web landing page lets you browse and search rich
templates and sample diagrams to help you design compelling and
professional-looking diagrams to represent your intent visually. You can
also see your recently accessed Visio files to resume editing
your diagram.

 

In this blog post, we discuss various ways to access the Visio for the
web landing page and other ways to quickly create new Visio diagrams.

 

**Note:** To create and edit a diagram in Visio for the web, you need to
have a subscription for Visio Plan 1 or Plan 2. For more information,
see [Compare Visio
versions](https://products.office.com/en-US/visio/microsoft-visio-plans-and-pricing-compare-visio-options).

 

**Using office.com**

The [www.office.com](http://www.office.com/) portal is the fastest and
easiest way to get started with the Visio for the web create and edit
experience. The Office.com app gallery provides a Visio tile. Clicking
that tile launches the landing page of Visio for the web. With the new
[www.office.com](http://www.office.com/) experience, if you can't find
the Visio tile in the gallery, click **Show more apps** to get access to
Visio.

Also from [www.office.com](http://www.office.com/) you can create a
blank Visio diagram, by clicking **New** and **Visio drawing**, and this
file is created in your OneDrive for Business by default.

 

**Using the Office 365 app launcher (also called the "waffle")**

You can switch to Visio for the web from Office.com (or other Office 365
web apps and services for which you have a license) after clicking the
app launcher (or "waffle") icon in the top menu. If the Visio tile is
not shown, click **All apps** to get to the Visio for the web landing
page.

**Using visio.office.com**

It's easy to forget or lose sight of stuff that you discover on the
internet, so it's important that you have a simple URL that you can
remember or bookmark in your browser. Visio for the web can be easily
accessed through the [visio.office.com](http://visio.office.com/) URL
which takes you to the landing page after you sign-in to your Office 365
account.

 

**Creating a new Visio drawing**

From your Sharepoint or OneDrive for Business document library, you can
create a blank Visio diagram with the **New** menu. With one click on
**Visio drawing**, a Visio diagram file is created in the document
library and the Visio app launches for creating and editing so you can
start the diagramming process.

**Opening an existing Visio drawing for editing**

From your SharePoint or OneDrive for Business document library, you can
open a **Visio drawing**, which will launch the Visio web app in view
mode. From here click on **Open diagram** in the menu and click on
**Edit in browser** to start editing the Visio diagram.

To learn more about Visio for the web, you can visit the [Visio product
page](https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/visio/flowchart-software).
From this page, click **Sign in \>** to launch the Visio landing page
after you sign-in to your Office 365 account.
