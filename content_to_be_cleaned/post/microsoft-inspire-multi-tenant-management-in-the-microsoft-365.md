Microsoft Inspire is the annual event where Microsoft joins the global
partner community from over 130 countries. It's also a chance to
recognize the many achievements of our partners. To learn more about
these outstanding achievements, check out the winners of our [Microsoft
2020 Partner of the Year
awards](https://blogs.partner.microsoft.com/mpn/congratulations-to-the-2020-microsoft-partner-of-the-year-awards-winners-and-finalists/?wt.mc_id=&cr_cc=OCPS_PRM_GL_INE_NN_NN_OO_LI_POTYA_announcement_blog).

 

This year's all-new virtual Inspire experience includes keynotes in
three time zones and interactive breakout sessions to enable us to
connect, learn, and collaborate with our partners. And as you might
expect, a lot of
[announcements](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/blog/2020/07/21/meeting-experiences-teams-security-capabilities-microsoft-365/)
around Microsoft 365.

 

In the Microsoft 365 admin center space, we talked about and
demonstrated several new features and improvements that are specifically
designed from the ground up for partners that manage multiple Office
365/Microsoft 365 tenants.

 

Timothy Heeney, a Principal Program Manager on the Microsoft 365 admin
center team starts things off with a [great
discussion](https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/partners/videos/inspire-m365-admin-multi-tenant-investments "Microsoft Inspire: Multi-tenant investments in the Microsoft 365 admin center")
of the investments we\'ve made and are making in the multi-tenant
management space.  Justin Lam and Vivek Raigaga, who are both Program
Managers on Tim\'s team, give us a [fantastic demo of the multi-tenant
features](https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/partners/videos/inspire-managing-multiple-m365-tenants "Microsoft Inspire: Multi-tenant features in the Microsoft 365 admin center")
we have introduced and are introducing, and a [fantastic demo of the
role-based access control (RBAC)
improvements](https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/partners/videos/inspire-rbac-in-m365-admin-center "Microsoft Inspire: RBAC improvements in the Microsoft 365 admin center")
we\'ve made.

 

We hope you enjoy these new features, and as always, please send us your
comments, feedback, questions, and suggestions.

 

\--The Microsoft 365 admin center team
